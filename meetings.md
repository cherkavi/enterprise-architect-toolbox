# meetings

## Planning (The "Before")
Effective meetings start before the calendar invite is sent.
*   **Clear Objective**: Define the "Definition of Done" for the meeting (e.g., "Approval of Auth Architecture").
*   **Agenda Design**: Break down technical topics into time-boxed segments. 
*   [console CLI application -  meeting time controller you can measure time of your meeting parts/points/chapters ](https://github.com/cherkavi/meeting-time-controller)

## Execution & Note-Taking (The "During")
### way of actively present on a meeting version 1
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/8113355/175804952-de19152d-397b-4cf5-9166-9b26f47bdc5f.png)  
split your notebook on left and right parts:
* **left**: capture what you hear, claims
* **right**: create/write your impressions
  * my feelings
  * what to learn
  * open questions 
*   **Parking Lot**: Use a "Parking Lot" for off-topic technical deep dives to keep the main meeting on track.

### way of actively present on a meeting - extension
> updated version of previous one
* Write down with red color "what you hear" ( capture ).  
* Write down with blue/pencil "what you think/feel" ( create )
* Capture _Why_ was Option A chosen over Option B?
* Create legend with list of attendees and use first letters of the surname as tags for claims for that persons

## after the meeting
Minutes should be distributed within 24 hours while the context is fresh.
* consider right part for action points
* create meeting minutes (till the EOD), highlight important points
  1. **Subject**: Date, Project Name, Participants (and absentees).
  2. **Discussion Points**: Brief summaries of the main arguments.
  3. **Action Items Table**: Use visual Tags/Icons to make the minutes scannable:
     *   `[DECISION]` - Finalized agreements.
     *   `[ACTION]` - Tasks to be completed.
     *   `[RISK]` - Potential issues identified.
     *   `[INFO]` - Important context or facts.

    |Item                            |Owner             |Deadline   |Status     |
    |:---                            |:---              |:---       |:---       |
    |[ACTION]Implement rate limiting |@dev_lead         |2023-10-25 |Open       |
    |[ACTION]Review security logs    |@security_analyst |2023-10-22 |In Progress|

  4. **Parking lot**: list of "off-topics" and "Any other business" (AOB)
