# people 

## people psycho types
> based on USSR.KGB input
- **narciss**  
  *detection*: ego-centric person  
  *connection*: say compliments, do not shadow him/her
- **defender**  
  *detection*: anti-narciss, closed for externs, stable, no changes  
  *connection*: common interests, need to spend time together  
- **action/leader**  
  *detection*: looking for strong competences  
  *connection*: do not say always YES, do not change them, be independent
- **soft**
  *detection*: emotional, ready to speak  
  *connection*: always in demand  to communicate with someone

## people society classification types
> based on USSR.KGB input
* condensator
  > has resources and entitled to use them
* center
  > has a lot of connections with different persons
* blocker
  > do not give you access to someone or ability to use something
