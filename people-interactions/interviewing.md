# Interviewing

## Types
- Case Interview
  > some situation, reaction on that situation
- Star interview
  > tell us about some past situation
- Projective/Vague question:
  > "what would someone do if ..."
  > open-end question
  > "why people build career ?"
- Closed questions / Quiz interview
  > only yes/no 

## Questions
- biggest achievement
- biggest setback 
- 3 positive qualities 
- 3 negative qualities

## Relax questions
- "how was your trip ?"

## Body position
- truth: eye contact
- experience: look ahead, diffusedly
